About Me

I am an economist with an M.Phil. in Applied Economics from the University of Karachi (2021), where I achieved a CGPA of 3.5. My professional journey includes serving as a Research Officer at OICCI, with specialization in Environmental and Health Economics.

My passion lies in addressing economic challenges through rigorous research, effective teaching, and meaningful policy engagement. I'm particularly dedicated to promoting inclusive growth, curriculum reform, and applied research that makes a tangible difference.
